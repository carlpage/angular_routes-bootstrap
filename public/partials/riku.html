Riku is one of the main characters in the Kingdom Hearts series. He lives on Destiny Islands with his best friends, Sora and Kairi, and all three of them dream of venturing from Destiny Islands to find out what lies beyond. Riku is five years old during Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep, fifteen during the events of Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories, sixteen during the events of Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days and Kingdom Hearts II, and seventeen during the events of Kingdom Hearts 3D: Dream Drop Distance. Riku's name is derived from the Japanese word for "land".
